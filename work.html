Our INVOLVEMENTS

“The Wind Beneath Our Wings”

PARTNERS • FUNDERS • NETWORKS

BirdLife International and Haribon Foundation have been working together since 1992 to conserve the Philippines’ unique and threatened biodiversity. The partnership has focused on the conservation of globally threatened bird species, the protection of Important Bird Areas, and the promotion of sustainable land use practices.

National Grid Corporation of the Philippines (NGCP) and Haribon Foundation have partnered to implement a forest restoration project called EMPOWER. The project aims to restore significant portions of three protected areas in the Philippines that have NGCP transmission facilities: The Pantabangan-Carranglan Watershed Forest Reserve (PCWFR) in Nueva Ecija, The Aurora Memorial National Park (AMNP) in Aurora, and The Bicol Natural Park (BNP) in Camarines Sur.

The Haribon Foundation has partnered with the European Union to train women leaders in Infanta, Quezon, despite the pandemic . The project is called Women Go! and it is part of the EU’s Spotlight Initiative. The initiative aims to eliminate all forms of violence against women and girls . The training program is designed to help women develop leadership skills, build self-confidence, and learn about sustainable development practices.
