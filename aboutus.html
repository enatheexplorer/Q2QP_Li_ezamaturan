About us

We are a pioneering non-governmental organization (NGO) based in the Philippines, dedicated to the preservation of the country's rich biodiversity and the sustainable management of its natural resources. You can check out the full video of our history in our YouTube Channel!

Established in 1972, Haribon is at the forefront of environmental conservation, engaging in scientific research, educational programs, and advocacy efforts to protect endangered species and their habitats. In 1995, the Haribon Foundation established a marine sanctuary dedicated to radiant seahorses. The location gained formal protection in 1998 and was recognized as the most exceptional marine protected area in the country by the MPA Support Network in 2007. We place a strong emphasis on community involvement, working collaboratively with local communities and fostering partnerships with various stakeholders. Haribon is committed to raising awareness, conducting research, and implementing on-the-ground initiatives to address environmental challenges and promote a harmonious relationship between humans and nature.

Vision

Haribon Foundation for the Conservation of Natural Resources or Haribon is a non-stock, non-profit organization working for the conservation of biodiversity in the Philippines. Its vision is to take the lead in caring for nature with the people, and for the people.

Mission

Haribon is a membership organization committed to promote the conservation of Philippine biodiversity and ecosystems through community-based and scientific approaches. We aim to raise awareness about environmental issues, engage local communities in conservation efforts, conduct research to better understand and protect biodiversity, and advocate for policies that support sustainable development and environmental protection.

Donation

The simplest way to get involved in conservation work is by donating to HARIBON® and the foundation’s programs. You can donate anytime, any amount, from anywhere in the world! Aside from being rewarded with a healthier environment, you can celebrate your contributions with a digital certificate* or an e-card* from HARIBON® that you could share online, give as a gift, or keep as a souvenir.

Membership

Advocating for the environment becomes a lot easier and exciting when you have a supportive community to guide you throughout your learning journey. For an annual donation, you can become a member of HARIBON® and gain knowledge from experts, get first dibs on volunteer opportunities, receive discounts, or simply connect with like-minded people in a safe space.

Volunteer

Did you know that you can invest in nature with your time and skills? HARIBON® needs your helping hand or artistic mind to breathe life into our conservation projects. Check our schedule in the homepage for more details!

Fundraising

Saving Philippine ecosystems and biodiversity now calls for even bigger commitments and rightfully empowered communities. Start a fundraising campaign or partnership with HARIBON® today to sustain the needs to care for our common home.
